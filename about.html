Why we built MediFind

Anyone who has looked for medicine in Zimbabwe knows the frustration — you travel across town to a pharmacy, only to find they don't stock what you need. You try another. Then another. Time, money, and energy wasted, often when you or a loved one is already unwell.

MediFind was built to solve this problem. By connecting pharmacies with patients through a simple, free platform, we make it possible to check medicine availability before making the trip. Pharmacies update their own stock in real time, and patients search for free.

Our goal is to make healthcare access more efficient across Zimbabwe — starting with Harare and growing from there.

For patients

Search any medicine and see which pharmacies near you have it in stock right now. Completely free, no account required.

For pharmacies

Register your pharmacy for free and let patients find you. Update your stock in real time from any device. Premium placement available.

Location-aware

Results are sorted by distance from your location so you always see the nearest pharmacy that has your medicine first.

Real-time data

Stock levels are updated directly by pharmacies. When a medicine runs out or comes back in, the information updates immediately.

Our values

01
Access for everyone
MediFind is free for patients and always will be. Healthcare information should not be behind a paywall.
02
Accuracy first
We encourage pharmacies to keep their stock updated in real time. We always remind patients to confirm availability before visiting.
03
Built for Zimbabwe
MediFind is designed around the realities of the Zimbabwean healthcare and pharmacy landscape — not copied from elsewhere.
04
Privacy respected
We do not sell patient data. Searches are not stored. Location is used only to calculate distances and never saved permanently.
