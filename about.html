Why we built PharmFinderz

Anyone who has looked for medicine in Zimbabwe knows the frustration — you travel across town to a pharmacy, only to find they don't stock what you need. You try another. Then another. Time, money, and energy wasted, often when you or a loved one is already unwell.

PharmFinderz was built to solve this problem. By connecting pharmacies with patients through a simple, free platform, we make it possible to check medicine availability before making the trip. Pharmacies update their own stock in real time, and patients search for free.

Our goal is to make healthcare access more efficient across Zimbabwe — starting with Harare and growing from there.

For patients

Search any medicine and see which pharmacies near you have it in stock. Free, no account needed.

For pharmacies

Register free and let patients find you. Update your stock in real time from any device.

Location-aware

Results sorted by distance from your location — nearest pharmacy with your medicine first.

Real-time data

Stock levels updated by pharmacies directly. When medicine runs out or comes back, it shows immediately.

Our values

01
Access for everyone
PharmFinderz is free for patients and always will be. Healthcare information should not be behind a paywall.
02
Accuracy first
We encourage pharmacies to keep their stock updated in real time. We always remind patients to confirm before visiting.
03
Built for Zimbabwe
PharmFinderz is designed around the realities of the Zimbabwean healthcare and pharmacy landscape.
04
Privacy respected
We do not sell patient data. Searches are not stored. Location is only used to calculate distances.
