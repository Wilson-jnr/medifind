Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

PharmFinderz Zimbabwe is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Who we are

PharmFinderz Zimbabwe ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at pharmfinderz.co.zw. We provide a free service that helps patients in Zimbabwe find medicines at local pharmacies.

2. Information we collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you use PharmFinderz:

  • Location data — If you allow it, we use your device's GPS to show how far pharmacies are. We do not store your location permanently.
  • Search queries — When you search for a medicine, we process that search to return results. We do not store your search history.
  • Pharmacy account data — If you register a pharmacy, we collect your pharmacy name, address, phone, email, hours, and coordinates. This is stored in our database.
  • Usage data — We may collect basic information about how the site is used, such as page visits and browser type, through analytics tools.

3. How we use your information

  • Display pharmacy locations and medicine availability to patients
  • Allow pharmacies to manage their stock listings
  • Improve the performance and usability of PharmFinderz
  • Communicate with registered pharmacies about their accounts

4. Advertising

PharmFinderz may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, visit How Google uses data from sites that use Google services.

5. Cookies

PharmFinderz uses cookies to support site functionality and advertising. By using this site, you consent to the use of cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect how the site works.

6. Third-party services

  • Supabase — Our database provider, storing pharmacy and medicine data securely.
  • Netlify — Our website hosting provider.
  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet — Used for interactive maps during pharmacy registration.
  • Google AdSense — Used to display relevant advertisements.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Pharmacy information (name, address, phone, hours, medicine listings) is publicly visible on PharmFinderz by design, as the purpose of the service is to help patients find medicines.

8. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Pharmacy account passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at admin@pharmfinderz.co.zw to exercise these rights.

10. Children's privacy

PharmFinderz is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of PharmFinderz after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at admin@pharmfinderz.co.zw or visit our Contact page.
