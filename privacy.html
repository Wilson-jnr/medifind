Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

MediFind Zimbabwe is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Who we are

MediFind Zimbabwe ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at illustrious-meringue-97ffdb.netlify.app. We provide a free service that helps patients in Zimbabwe find medicines at local pharmacies.

2. Information we collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you use MediFind:

  • Location data — If you allow it, we use your device's GPS to show you how far away pharmacies are. We do not store your location permanently.
  • Search queries — When you search for a medicine, we process that search to return results. We do not store your search history.
  • Pharmacy account data — If you register a pharmacy, we collect your pharmacy name, address, phone number, email address, operating hours, and coordinates. This information is stored in our database.
  • Usage data — Like most websites, we may collect basic information about how the site is used, such as page visits and browser type, through analytics tools.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Display pharmacy locations and medicine availability to patients
  • Allow pharmacies to manage their stock listings
  • Improve the performance and usability of MediFind
  • Communicate with registered pharmacies about their accounts

4. Advertising

MediFind may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, visit How Google uses data from sites that use Google services.

5. Cookies

MediFind uses cookies to support site functionality and advertising. By using this site, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect how the site works.

6. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Our database provider, which stores pharmacy and medicine data securely.
  • Netlify — Our website hosting provider.
  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet — Used to display interactive maps during pharmacy registration.
  • Google AdSense — Used to display relevant advertisements.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Pharmacy information (name, address, phone number, hours, and medicine listings) is publicly visible on MediFind by design, as the purpose of the service is to help patients find medicines.

8. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Pharmacy account passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at admin@medifind.co.zw.

10. Children's privacy

MediFind is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered pharmacies of significant changes by email. Continued use of MediFind after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@medifind.co.zw or visit our Contact page.
